About Mediplus Diagnostics

Mediplus Diagnostics, established in 2012, is a trusted medical laboratory offering a comprehensive range of diagnostic services. Since our inception, we have been committed to providing accurate and timely results to our patients. Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped to perform various tests including Sugar, Blood Pressure, Total Cholesterol, Lipid Profile, Kidney Function Test (RFT), Liver Function Test (LFT), RA Factor, CRP, Blood R/E, and Urine R/E.
We prioritize patient care and strive to deliver excellence in diagnostics, ensuring the well-being and health of our community.
